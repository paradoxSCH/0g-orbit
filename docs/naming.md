# Naming

## Recommended Name

### 0G Orbit

Why it works:

- Short and memorable.
- Directly connected to zero gravity through the idea of orbit.
- Implies bounded autonomy: agents can move, but stay inside a controlled path.
- Fits the product: an agent wallet that allows action without unlimited freedom.
- Easier to say in a demo than a long infrastructure name.

Suggested tagline:

> Keep AI agents in orbit, not in custody.

Submission one-liner:

> 0G Orbit is a self-custodial operational wallet that lets AI agents spend and execute only within user-defined policies, with verifiable receipts on 0G.

## Good Alternatives

### ZeroGuard

- Strong safety signal.
- Related to 0G through Zero.
- Slightly more generic than 0G Orbit.

### 0G Halo

- Very short and visual.
- Suggests a protective boundary around agents.
- Slightly softer and less operational than Orbit.

### OrbitKey

- Implies keyless/bounded wallet control.
- Less directly tied to 0G unless paired with 0G branding.

### 0G Circuit

- Good if the product leans into circuit breakers and risk checks.
- More Track 2 / security sounding.

## Names to Avoid

- AgentSafe: too generic and normal.
- 0G Sentry / Sentri-like names: too close to an existing gallery project.
- 0G Marketplace / AgentMarket: crowded category signal.
- 0G Wallet: too plain and broad.
- 0G Tether: memorable but creates unnecessary token-brand confusion.
