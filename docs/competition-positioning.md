# Competition Positioning

## Recommended Track

Primary: Track 3 — Agentic Economy & Autonomous Applications.

Specific official direction: Operational Tools, especially self-custodial agent wallets and AI-governed operational infrastructure.

Secondary relevance: Track 2 if the risk-attestation module is used for high-risk DeFi or trading operations.

## Main Differentiator

0G Orbit is not an agent marketplace and not a trading agent. It is a bounded execution wallet for agents.

The strongest line:

> Autonomous agents should not hold private keys. They should hold limited, revocable, auditable authority.

## Why It Can Score

### 0G Technical Integration Depth & Innovation

- 0G Chain is used for actual enforcement, not just proof anchoring.
- 0G Storage stores full receipts and policy snapshots.
- 0G Compute can produce or verify operation plans.
- Agent ID creates an identity layer for delegated execution.

### Technical Implementation & Completeness

- The MVP has concrete contracts, policy checks, frontend flow, and repeatable demo operations.
- Rejections are as important as successful execution, which makes the product feel real.

### Product Value & Market Potential

- Every agent economy needs controlled spending and operation authority.
- The pattern applies to DAOs, AI SaaS teams, creator teams, data buyers, and future agent-to-agent workflows.

### User Experience & Demo Quality

- A judge can understand the demo without knowing trading strategy math.
- The dashboard can show green allowed operations and red rejected operations clearly.

### Team Capability & Documentation

- A threat model and test cases will help the project look serious.
- Clear local reproduction steps are mandatory.

## Crowded Areas to Avoid

- Agent marketplace first-screen UI.
- Generic AI assistant or chatbot.
- AI trading signal dashboard.
- Yield optimizer.
- Pure document proof app.
- Data marketplace.

## Suggested Project Description

0G Orbit is a self-custodial operational wallet for AI agents. Users and DAOs can authorize an Agent ID to execute only within bounded policies such as spend caps, recipient allowlists, function allowlists, cooldowns, and risk-attestation requirements. Every operation creates a verifiable receipt using 0G Chain and 0G Storage, enabling practical autonomous operations without surrendering custody.
